Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers to the most common questions about Unity Trust Bank, online banking, security, and accounts.

Is Unity Trust Bank a secure online bank?

Yes. Unity Trust Bank uses advanced encryption, multi-factor authentication, and continuous monitoring to protect customer data and transactions.

How do I open an account?

You can open an account online by clicking the “Get Started” or “Register” button and completing the sign-up form with your personal details.

Can I access my account on mobile?

Yes. Unity Trust Bank works on mobile phones, tablets, and desktops, allowing you to manage your finances anytime, anywhere.

Are there any hidden charges?

No. We believe in transparency. All applicable fees are clearly disclosed before you complete any transaction.

How do I contact customer support?

You can contact us via email, phone, or through the Contact Us page. Our support team is available 24/7 to assist you.

Is my money insured?

Customer funds are protected using industry-standard security practices. Additional insurance coverage applies based on account type and location.
